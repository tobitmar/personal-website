About Me

My name is Margaret Ologban, I am currently a student at the university of Texas at Dallas who majors in Computer Science. I am planning on going into the cybersecurity branch of Computer Science, while developing my skills in languages such as C++, Java, and more.

I am an upcoming eSports editor for the award winning student media publication, The Mercury, for UT Dallas. In my free time I enjoy playing video games and listening to various types of music.

I was born in Austin, Texas, and grew up in central Texas. I moved to the Dallas-Fort Worth area for college, and I have been enjoying my time here! I like going out to eat in Carrolton, Texas the most.

I hope to work on projects using my future and present skills. Cybersecurity is a big interest of mine, but also working on websites and back-end coding is something I am open to as well as I go further in my education. I hope we work together well in the future.
